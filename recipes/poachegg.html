How to poach an egg

alright fuckers today we're doing eggs

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • water
  • white vinegar

How to

  1. boil your water in a pot with a fuckload of vinegar (it won't affect taste)
  2. once water is at a rolling boil, reduce heat so that a few bubbles are breaking the surface
  3. make a whirpool in the pot and crack your eggs into the water, cook for 3 minutes max
  4. retrieve your eggs from the water and let them sit for about 30 seconds, season to taste
  5. eat your protein
  6. make some hollandaise too that shit is bomb with poached eggs
